    • WHAT STYLE ARE YOU LOOKING FOR?

    If you want to go to for more sexy look then a backless wedding dress is for you. This style shows off your whole back and give you a look that is daring. People are sometimes hesitant for this because it may be too scandalous, however if you keep the front of the dress more elegant and conservative then the backless portion will balance it out. This backless style has become a popular trend for younger people however any age can rock this look. Just make sure that it is a good fit to your back; we dont want anything hanging loose.

    If you want something flirty then the off the shoulder look is for you. This style look good on all age groups however if you are older then I recomend this look. It is perfect because it doesnt releal too much but just reveals enough

    Here is a elegant dress. If want to look more elegant and less sexy then find a dress with less lace and more white. White make you seem like you are not trying as hard but as the same time you look amazing. Everyone appreciates an elegant dress

    If are having a small wedding and dont want to spend thousands on your dress then a simple dress is the way to go. The more jewels on the dress then the more expessive it is. Going with an elegant dress ensure the best price and a great look.
