    • FIND FOR YOUR WEDDING

    Wedding picture always look best outside. This is because it will provide the best lighting and as well as all the natural beautiful colors of nature. It is also important that when you take pictures for your wedding always try to look natural and show your love for eachother.

    As far as decorations goes, it is a great idea to have your wedding outside. An outside wedding makes for a magical night. Just hang up some lights and it will be like dancing under the stars.

    There are two options when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle. You can either wear your hair down or in a fancy bun. I always recommend wearing your hair down. Whether it is straight or wavy, hair down is the way to go. When you have your hair in a bun there is a chance it may fall out although it is a very fancy look

    In regards to makeup it is a smart things to go light on the eyes. You want to have a natural beauty to your face. You want makeup that will show the real you.

    Flowers are a great way to turn your wedding into something magical. However the color of the flower could be tricky because you do not want to have something too colorful. It is best when chossing the color of the flowers, go with more neutral colors.
